The gamma distribution is defined as 

&&
\begin{align*}
    f_X(x; \alpha, \lambda)=\begin{cases}
               \frac{\lambda^\alphax^{\alpha-1}e^{-\lambda x}}{\Gamma(\alpha)} &x>0\\
               0 &otherwise
            \end{cases}
\end{align*}
&&

and it has 

&&
\begin{align*}
    EX = \frac{\alpha}{\lambda} && Var(X) = \frac{\alpha}{\lambda^2}
\end{align*}
